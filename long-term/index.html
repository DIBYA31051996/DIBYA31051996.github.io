Long-Term Monitoring and Forecast

Long-Term Monitoring & Forecast

A compact directory of long-term monitoring, forecast, and archival resources useful for solar activity context, space-weather tracking, and multi-cycle reference work.

SolarMonitor

This resource combines near-real-time and archive views of active regions, events, images, and solar-weather context products in one place.

Open resource

HelioViewer

HelioViewer provides quick visual access to multi-mission solar imagery with interactive browsing, overlays, and movie generation for event context.

Open resource

SIDC-SILSO

The SILSO sunspot-number database provides daily, monthly, and long-term indices that are central to solar-cycle monitoring and reconstruction work.

Open resource

Marshall Solar Cycle Forecast

Solar-cycle progression plots and forecast context from operational space-weather resources, useful for broad cycle-phase interpretation.

Open resource

Space Weather Prediction Center

A comprehensive source of operational space-weather data, products, alerts, forecast summaries, and environment maps.

Open resource

Australian Space Weather Database

Australian space-weather products, reports, and event summaries for operational monitoring and archive reference.

Open resource

Canadian Space Weather Database

Canadian geospace and space-weather observations, services, and data access for geomagnetic and radio-environment context.

Open resource

Geomagnetic Kp Index

The definitive Kp and ap geomagnetic indices for monitoring magnetospheric variability and solar-terrestrial conditions.

Open resource

Solar Active Region Compilation

Daily and historical active-region listings with identifiers and compact summaries useful for event cross-reference work.

Open resource

Solar Flare Catalog Compilation

Flare listings and long-baseline event compilations that support event-based studies and historical comparisons.

Open resource

Other Resources

GONG Data Archive

Synoptic full-disk observations and long-running helioseismic products from the GONG network.

Live Space Weather Status

A broad operational dashboard for solar, heliospheric, and geospace context.

Solar Dynamo Dataverse

A collective of databases and products tailored to understanding and predicting the solar cycle.

Solar Activity Reconstruction

Solar cyclic activity over the last millennium reconstructed from annual proxy records.

SMARPs and SHARPs

Active-region data products supporting machine-learning studies and solar cycle 23-24 investigations.

VizieR Online Data Catalog

Historic solar and astrophysical catalogs, including sunspot, faculae, and prominence datasets.

Sunspot Observations in 1719-1720

Early telescopic observations that help extend the long-baseline record of solar activity.

SOLIS Data Product

Integrated synoptic magnetogram and dopplergram products from the SOLIS program.
